1. Home Staging That Works: Sell Your Home in Less Time for More Money

A book written by Starr C. Osborne opens secrets of professional staging companies to the readers. She has gathered insights from the best American staging experts and teaches how to make your property appealing to buyers. There are hints on improving the curb appeal, driving interest on the web, affordable ways to clean and repair a home as well as strategies that will change every room of the home for better.

2. Home Staging: The Winning Way to Sell Your House for More Money

Barn Schwarz with Mary Seehafer Sears has prepared a wonderful book that tells what home staging is. It teaches how to accentuate the benefits of the home and improve its presentation.

A book is full of organizing tips and checklists as well as photographs of the property before and after staging.

3. Psychological staging

Written by Kristie Barnett, a famous designer and the Decorologist website host, a book is compiled of her ideas and knowledge on this topic. There are various pieces of advice on staging rooms, using styles and decorative materials in staging. The emphasis is made on the sales psychology and there are answers to the questions what buyers want and how to provide them with that.

4. Do’s and Don’ts in Home Staging and Redesign

A practical guide from the founder of the Academy of Staging and Redesign Barbara Jennings is incredibly popular among readers. There are 101 case studies that will help sellers to avoid home staging mistakes. She also offers rules and explains psychology of home staging providing images of rooms before home staging has taken place and after that.

5. Feel at Home: Home Staging Secrets for a Quick and Easy Sell

Tori Toth, the author of the book, opens readers some secret practices to use in your own home. A book written by an experienced home designer Andy Capelluto contains practical pieces of advice for homeowners who prepare their property for sale.
