Virtual reality has become an indispensable helper in many industries. Two of them are real estate and interior design. It is not surprising today to see pictures of perfectly designed rooms that look appealing and highly functional. If you think that most of them exist in reality, you are mistaken. The biggest number of these images are virtually staged. An architect or designer takes any empty room, reproduces it with the help of modern software and stages it with Randy furniture and accessories.

This furniture placing service has gained popularity nowadays. Firstly, it is ordered by people who sell the property that is absolutely empty. Real estate agents have already appreciated the impact furniture placement has on the buyers as they are more attracted by staged property and it is sold faster. Only 10% of people are capable of visualising how the home will look like with furniture. The rest of them would prefer to see this transformation with their own eyes and virtual furniture placement is a way out in this case.

The other group of professionals who take advantage of virtual staging are interior designers. Today they do not make paper sketches – it is much more comfortable to perform all the measurements and fit the really-existing furniture pieces using the latest software. As a result, they get a complete picture and can make amendments anytime to achieve the best look and functionality.

How virtual furniture placement takes place

Today it is not surprising to see 3D renderings of furniture for sale. The largest companies offer their furniture models online allowing to use them in your projects. Of course, it can be free or you may need to buy these models to be able to use them. This process is a kind of the marketing solution that brings furniture of one or another brand into masses. Professional virtual staging companies have huge sets of such models to meet the requirements of the most demanded customers, so they can create real interior design masterpieces using their computers. The procedure of furniture placement is quite time-consuming and painstaking.

You should complete the following stages:

  1. Make the plan of the room with accurate measurements of its parts.
  2. Choose the style of the interior design.
  3. Create a suitable finish on walls, ceiling and floor.
  4. Select furniture pieces to meet the style and playing together well.
  5. Distribute them around the room choosing the necessary angles.
  6. Consider the light source and add shades.
  7. Add decor and accessories.
